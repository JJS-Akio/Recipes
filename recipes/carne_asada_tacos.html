Carne Asada Tacos

Carne asada tacos are a tantalizing feast of grilled, marinated beef packed into soft tortillas and adorned with your favorite toppings like fresh cilantro, diced onions, and a squeeze of lime. Each bite delivers a smoky flavor from the grill, enhanced by the zestiness of lime and the richness of avocado, making them irresistibly delicious. Easy to cook and fun to assemble, these tacos offer a versatile meal that brings the vibrant spirit of Mexican street food right to your table, perfect for any casual gathering or family dinner.

Ingredients

  • Flank or skirt steak (about 2 pounds)
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Fresh lime juice
  • Orange juice
  • Olive oil
  • Chopped fresh cilantro
  • Ground cumin
  • Chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Corn or flour tortillas
  • Optional: sliced avocado, salsa, diced onions, chopped cilantro, and lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Combine garlic, lime juice, orange juice, olive oil, cilantro, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl to create the marinade.
  2. Place the steak in a sealable bag or shallow dish and pour the marinade over it. Ensure the meat is well-coated, then refrigerate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.
  3. Preheat a grill to high heat. Remove the steak from the marinade and grill it for about 5-7 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or longer depending on desired doneness.
  4. Let the steak rest for a few minutes after grilling, then slice it against the grain into thin strips.
  5. Warm the tortillas on the grill for a few seconds on each side.
  6. Assemble the tacos by placing slices of steak in the tortillas, topped with onions, cilantro, avocado, and a squeeze of lime juice.
  7. Serve immediately with additional lime wedges and salsa on the side.

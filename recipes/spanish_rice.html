Spanish Rice

Spanish rice, a vibrant and savory side dish, brings a touch of Spanish cuisine to your table with its rich tomato flavor and a hint of smoky paprika. The dish is beautifully simple to prepare, combining rice with onions, garlic, and spices, simmered in a savory broth until fluffy and aromatic. Ideal for pairing with a variety of meals, this versatile and colorful rice adds a festive flair to any dinner, ensuring a delightful culinary experience with every spoonful.

Ingredients

  • Long-grain white rice (1 cup)
  • Vegetable oil or olive oil (2 tablespoons)
  • Garlic cloves, minced (2 cloves)
  • Onion, finely chopped (1 medium)
  • Tomato sauce (8 ounces) or diced tomatoes
  • Chicken or vegetable broth (2 cups)
  • Frozen peas and carrots (1/2 cup, optional)
  • Ground cumin (1 teaspoon)
  • Paprika (1 teaspoon)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic, saut√©ing until the onions are translucent.
  2. Stir in the rice, cooking until it is lightly toasted and golden, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the tomato sauce (or diced tomatoes), broth, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to the skillet, and bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  5. If using, add the peas and carrots in the last 5 minutes of cooking.
  6. Remove from heat and let it sit covered for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork before serving.
  7. Garnish with chopped cilantro or parsley if desired.
